About this app


Created for musics lovers, this project allows users to keep tracks of their favorite artists and their songs. By just logging in, you can add to or update your personal collection of favorites artists and songs. You can also discover more artists, or change your stored collection as your preferences change over time.

Thanks to Spotify's API, you can enjoy links to any song in you collection as well as see the album art for the record. This is your a digital record collection, providing album arts, song info, and media links at the click of a button.

Have you ever heard of a song and couldn't remember who the artist was or that song's name? This app will help you and also help you keep up with new and old artist as they produce new material. Want to get the details on your favorite songs from years ago that brings you back to that special place? This is the app for you!

There are always new songs coming out, summer hits that come and go, and we forget some of the best songs too easily. I hope this app make it easier to keep track of our favorite artists and keeps our favorite artists songs up to date.
