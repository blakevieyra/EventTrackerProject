About this app


Created for musics lovers, this project allows users to keep tracks of their favorite artists and their songs. By logging in, you can add to or update your personal collection of favorites artists and songs. You can discover more artists, or change the collection as your preferences change over time.
Have you ever heard of a song and couldn't remember who the artist was or that song's name? This app will help you and also help you keep up with new and old artist as they produce new material. Want to get the details on your favorite songs from years ago that brings you back to that special place? This is the app for you!
